You've already done the exploration. Stop and judge it against the direction before acting.

1. **Where we are** — what the investigation has actually established (one short paragraph, concrete findings only — not "we looked at X").
2. **Where we're going** — the decision, plan, or next action this research is meant to support.
3. **Verdict** — is the current research sufficient for that next step?
   - **Sufficient** — name the load-bearing facts and proceed.
   - **More needed** — list only the unknowns that would actually change the next action, and what to do to close each.

Bias toward "sufficient." A gap is worth flagging only if answering it would change the next move — not just because more could be known.
